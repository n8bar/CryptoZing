Prove the repaired addresses belong to the configured watch-only wallet, confirm the watcher still processes representative repaired invoices, and preserve the evidence needed to close a payment-critical bug milestone.

## Historical boundary

This verification used seed/test data in the pre-production environment, not customer funds. It proved the immediate branch correction and watcher compatibility; it did not prove safety under later wallet-key rotation or unsupported address reuse.
