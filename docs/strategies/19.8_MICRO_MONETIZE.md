# MS19 Phase 8 Strategy — Micro-Monetize

Status: **Stub — not started.** Scope undecided; brainstorm before flesh-out. Not approved implementation direction.
Parent milestone doc: [`docs/milestones/19_OB_HARDENING_OPS.md`](../milestones/19_OB_HARDENING_OPS.md)

> ⚠️ **Before starting this phase:** re-enable the `frontend-design` plugin (disabled 2026-06-23) — it's needed for the donation-page UI work.

**Goal:** Stand up CryptoZing's first revenue surface — a donation page — as a low-stakes entry into monetization. Makes the "leave room for monetization" posture concrete without committing to fees or paid tiers.

## Open scope question (resolve before flesh-out)
The size of this phase — and whether it stays in MS19 or spins out to its own milestone — hinges on:
- **BTC donations** — native; route to a CryptoZing wallet address. No bank account involved.
- **Fiat donations** — a live consideration. Needs a payment processor **and** the AFCU business account to land/convert. Larger surface, more legal/tax weight.

Decide BTC-only vs. BTC+fiat first. Fiat in scope ⇒ this likely wants its own milestone rather than an MS19 phase.
